# WI-ME-06 — Fan mount

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Track | Mechanical |
| Milestone | M5-06 |
| Depends on | WI-ME-01 |
| Spec refs | §7.4, §8.7 |
| Status | Not started |

## Objective

Design a guarded, vibration-isolated fan mount that provides gentle canopy circulation without
blasting seedlings or coupling noise into the frame.

## Deliverables

- [ ] CAD/STL for 80/92 mm fan mount with rubber grommets and a required guard.
- [ ] Placement giving circulation, not a direct drying stream at the seedling (§7.4).
- [ ] Optional removable intake filter.
- [ ] Avoids resonant thin panels; meets acoustic intent (§8.7).

## Acceptance criteria

- Guarded and isolated (spec §15.6 M5-06).
- Supports the noise targets validated in [WI-QA-04](../05-validation-qa/WI-QA-04-acoustic.md).
