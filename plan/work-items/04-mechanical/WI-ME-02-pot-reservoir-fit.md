# WI-ME-02 — Pot & reservoir selection and fit

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Track | Mechanical |
| Milestone | M5-02 |
| Depends on | WI-ME-01 |
| Spec refs | §5.5, §7.7, §16.2 |
| Status | Not started |

## Objective

Select and model the pot and reservoir, proving tool-free removal and correct drainage/overflow.

## Deliverables

- [ ] Pot: 8–10 L compact (12–19 L full-yield), drain-capable, removable without disconnecting wires.
- [ ] Reservoir: 2.5–4 L compact (4–6 L full-yield), food-safe, tool-free removal, hand-cleanable.
- [ ] CAD models + BOM entries; overflow path routes to leak tray, not electronics (§7.7).
- [ ] Pot must not sit in stagnant water unless an intentional air-gap self-watering design.

## Acceptance criteria

- Fit verified in assembly (spec §15.6 M5-02).
- Reservoir/pot removable per serviceability rules (§8.4).
