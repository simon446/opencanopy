## Objective

Model the complete open-frame vertical-stack assembly within the locked compact envelope, with all
modules placed and the wet/dry zone separation respected.

## Deliverables

- [ ] Source CAD + STEP for the full assembly (`mechanical/cad/source/`, `step/`).
- [ ] Open-frame vertical stack: upper dry bay, LED zone, grow zone, lower pot, bottom wet bay (§8.1).
- [ ] Within compact envelope 450–500 × 300–350 × 650–750 mm; visual language per §3.4.
- [ ] CAD verification checklist (§12.1): insert/remove paths, clearances, center of gravity with full
      reservoir + plant, drip/leak path.

## Acceptance criteria

- All modules placed (spec §15.6 M5-01); envelope within §3.3 compact target.
- "Water fails downward; electronics live upward" rule (§6.2) visibly satisfied.
