## Objective

Write `docs/safety.md` covering water/electricity, thermal, food/contact, and child/pet safety.

## Deliverables

- [ ] Water/electricity rules: external certified PSU, no AC mains inside, drip loops, leak lockout,
      pump-fail-off (§17.1).
- [ ] Thermal safety: LED derating, tested materials, no combustibles on heat sink (§17.2).
- [ ] Food/contact: cleanable reservoir, avoid leaching plastics, DIY-not-certified disclaimer (§17.3).
- [ ] Child/pet: fan guard, no sharp metal/impeller access, capsaicin handling warning (§17.4).

## Acceptance criteria

- Safety guide complete (§21); cross-checked against the [WI-QA-08](../05-validation-qa/WI-QA-08-electrical-safety.md) sign-off.
