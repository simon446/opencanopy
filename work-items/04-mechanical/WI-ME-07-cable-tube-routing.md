# WI-ME-07 — Cable & tube routing

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Track | Mechanical |
| Milestone | M5-07 |
| Depends on | WI-ME-03, WI-ME-04 |
| Spec refs | §8.5 |
| Status | Not started |

## Objective

Design cable channels and tube routing that separate wiring from water, force drip loops before
electronics, and keep tubing inspectable.

## Deliverables

- [ ] CAD for cable channel separating wires from tubes.
- [ ] Drip loops + grommets at every electronics-bay entry; no cable enters from directly below without one.
- [ ] Strain relief at all removable/moving modules; tubing visible/inspectable for leaks.
- [ ] Labels for pump, fan, LED, moisture, reservoir, leak matching [WI-EE-05](../03-electronics/WI-EE-05-harness-pinout.md).

## Acceptance criteria

- Drip loops and clips present (spec §15.6 M5-07).
- Routing matches the harness table labels.
